Two-day ECS Event at 2024 A-RIP Planning Workshop

July 20 – 21, 2024

The APARC Reanalysis Intercomparison Project (A-RIP) is sponsoring a 2-day Early-Career Scientist (ECS) event from July 20 to 21 on the University of Colorado Boulder campus (building/room TBD). The A-RIP ECS event is scheduled to follow the 2024 Quadrennial Ozone Symposium (QOS) in Boulder, Colorado, USA (July 15 to 19), which will attract climate researchers from around the world.

We are targeting Early-Career Scientists (i.e. BSc, MSc and PhD students, as well as postdoctoral fellows) from all over the world who plan to attend the QOS and A-RIP meetings in Boulder, along with local students and researchers. Participation may be limited (we anticipate approximately 10 places). ECS applications from the Global South are particularly encouraged. The ECS event is organized by Mohamadou Diallo (A-RIP ECS and outreach coordinator), Sean Davis (local organizer), V. Lynn Harvey (local organizer), Gloria Manney (A-RIP co-lead), Jonathon Wright (A-RIP co-lead), and Masatomo Fujiwara (A-RIP co-lead).

Logistical details

Participation in the ECS event is free of charge. Coffee breaks and lunches are also included for the first day (July 20). Some financial assistance is available for travel support (e.g., for those who need to cover the cost of flight changes or extra nights' lodging) for a limited number of ECSs.

The deadline for applications requesting travel support is June 24, 2024. Applications not requesting travel support will close when enrollment is full (watch this space) but must be received by July 8, 2024.

For all applications, please send an email to A-RIP (aripworkshop2024@gmail.com) with the subject “ECS Event Application” and include a CV as well as information on your background, university, country of origin, and how you think this event will help you in your current or future projects. Please specify whether you require a visa and whether you are also planning to attend the main A-RIP Workshop.

The A-RIP Workshop

The A-RIP workshop will be held following the ECS event, from July 22 to 24, 2024. The workshop will be held at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP) Space Science Center (SPSC). Please see the preliminary programme for the A-RIP workshop.

The ECS event will prioritize informal, small group, and one-on-one activities to bring ECS together with both other ECS from diverse regions and backgrounds and senior scientists.
